2. Database Schema (Tables)
We need to design the tables to hold the data for the public site and admin dashboard.

admins: To handle the single admin account login.
Columns: id, email, password_hash, created_at.
workers: To store workforce registration submissions.
Columns: id, first_name, last_name, email, phone, skill_area, experience_years, skills_details, cv_file_path, created_at.
gallery: To manage media across Projects, Workers, Products, and Community.
Columns: id, category, title, image_path, file_size, uploaded_at.
blog_posts: To handle news and updates.
Columns: id, title, category, content, image_path, status (Draft/Published), created_at, updated_at.
products: To track ecosystem products and services.
Columns: id, name, brand (RAW HUB, Mi Boo, Mi Look), image_path, status (Active/Draft), created_at.
reviews: To handle customer feedback.
Columns: id, customer_name, email, rating, review_text, status (Pending/Approved), created_at.
rewards: To manually track customer reward claims.
Columns: id, customer_name, reward_type, points_value, status (Pending/Completed), created_at.
